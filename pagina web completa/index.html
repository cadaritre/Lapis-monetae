What is Lapis Monetae?

Lapis Monetae is a next-generation digital currency inspired by timeless materials — clean whites, subtle textures, and refined curves — built to be as elegant in design as it is robust in technology.

Hybrid Consensus

A thoughtful union of Proof of Work and Proof of Authority for balanced security, decentralization, and governance.
